Our Programmes

Tailored Support for Every Child and Family

For Children

Literacy Clubs

Our literacy clubs make reading fun and engaging, helping children develop strong foundational reading skills through interactive activities and age-appropriate books.

Learning Through Games

We use educational games to teach complex concepts in an enjoyable way, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration.

SEND-Friendly Sessions

These sessions are specifically designed to cater to children with Special Educational Needs and Disabilities (SEND), providing a supportive environment with visual aids and quiet spaces to ensure every child can thrive.

Accessibility Note: Quiet spaces available, visual aids used, flexible pacing.

Cultural Identity Workshops

These workshops help children connect with their Senegambian heritage through stories, music, art, and language, fostering a strong sense of identity and pride.

For Parents

Parenting for Inclusion

Workshops designed to equip parents with strategies to create an inclusive home environment and advocate for their children's needs.

Supporting Education at Home

Practical tools and tips for parents to effectively support their children's learning and academic development outside of school hours.

Understanding Cultural Conflict

Sessions to help parents navigate the complexities of cultural differences within the family and community, fostering harmony and understanding.

SEND Awareness Workshops

These workshops raise awareness about Special Educational Needs and Disabilities, helping parents understand various conditions and how to seek appropriate support.

In Senegal

Educator Training

We provide comprehensive training for educators in Senegal, focusing on inclusive teaching methodologies, early identification of learning difficulties, and creating supportive classroom environments.

Parent Workshops

Tailored workshops for parents in Senegal to empower them with knowledge and tools to support their children's development, especially those with SEND needs, within their cultural context.

SEND Resource Pack Samples

We offer samples of our specialized SEND resource packs, providing practical materials and guides for both educators and parents to implement inclusive practices.

Striving for Inclusion

Striving for Inclusion is a community-led organisation dedicated to empowering children and families from Senegambian backgrounds through inclusive education, cultural identity, and family support.
