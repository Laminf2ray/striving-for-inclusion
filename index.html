Empowering Families, Embracing Inclusion

Striving for Inclusion exists to empower children and families through inclusive education, cultural identity, and family support. We provide SEND-friendly reading activities, learning games, and workshops for children while equipping parents with practical tools to support their children's development, manage cultural differences at home, and better understand neurodiversity.

Nurturing Every Child, Empowering Every Family

The purpose of Striving for Inclusion is to create a supportive, culturally aware, and inclusive learning environment where children, especially those from Senegalese and Gambian backgrounds, can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We focus on empowering both children and their families, particularly those with SEND needs, through accessible education, cultural understanding, and active community engagement. At Striving for Inclusion, we believe education goes beyond the classroom; we inspire curiosity, creativity and a stronger sense of identity.

Our Key Programmes

For Children

Engaging literacy clubs, fun learning games, and cultural workshops designed for every child, including SEND-friendly sessions.

For Parents

Workshops and resources to help parents support their children's education, understand cultural differences, and navigate SEND needs.

In Senegal

Training for educators and parent workshops in Senegal, providing essential SEND resources and inclusive practices.
