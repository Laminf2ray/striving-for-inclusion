Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do your programmes cater to?

Our programmes are designed for children primarily aged 5-16, with specific activities tailored for different age ranges. We also offer extensive workshops and resources for parents and educators.

Are your services only for Senegambian families?

While we have a strong focus on supporting Senegambian communities, our inclusive education principles and many resources are beneficial for all families and children, regardless of their background.

How can I register for a workshop?

You can register for our workshops directly on our Events & Workshops page. Each event listing includes a registration form or a link to register.

Do you offer support for specific SEND conditions?

Our SEND-friendly sessions and resources are designed to be adaptable and beneficial for a wide range of Special Educational Needs and Disabilities. We aim to provide general support and guidance, and can also direct you to more specialized resources where appropriate.
